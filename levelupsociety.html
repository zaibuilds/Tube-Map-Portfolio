    Intro:

    This was the first hackathon I participated in alongside two of my friends. The theme of this hackathon was to level up society through gamification. We were given a selection of categories to choose from; Better Mental Health, Better Security, Better Finance, Better Retail and Better Learning.

    My friends and I chose to go for Better Retail; creating a concept to reward users for consistency within their skincare routine as well as leaving product reviews.

    Process:

    Initially, we began the process of designing this product by ideating which features we would include as well as the information architecture.

    In total, we created 3 iterations of this information architecture map before reaching the final version.

    With each iteration of the information architecture map we slightly reduced the scope of the project in order to be able to hit the deadline.

    The features we decided on were the following:

    Dashboard page with user information, progress bar and upcoming benefits

    Routine page showcasing skincare products in a users routine as well as key information such as date of the start of use, consistency (logged via calendar) and a share button

    Log Entry page for users to record skin behaviour as well as other factors such as diet and mood.
